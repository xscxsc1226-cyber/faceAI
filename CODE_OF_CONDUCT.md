# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on ideas and technical details, not individuals.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional community.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate this code.
